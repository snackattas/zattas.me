About Me


I'm Zach Attas. Welcome to my site.

A bit about me: I love to experiment. When cooking, I'll always have a new new batch of fermented <insert esoteric vegetable> on hand. When I decided to pursue coding, I enrolled in Udacity's Intro to Programming Nanodegree. Soon after I found myself enrolled in Udacity's Full Stack Web Developer Nanodegree.

However, I'd say only %50 percent of my learning came from those courses. The rest is from a random assortment of MOOC curricula, online textbooks, stack overflow, podcasts, blogposts, code documentation, etc. Self-guided learning is my forte.
